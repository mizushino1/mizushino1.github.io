What is DOTA 2?

DOTA 2 is a highly competitive, free-to-play Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Valve. It is the standalone sequel to the community-created mod Defense of the Ancients for Warcraft III.

Core Gameplay

At its heart, the game is a 5v5 strategic battle. Each player controls a single "Hero" with unique abilities and playstyles. The primary goal is to destroy the Ancient, a massive structure located within the enemy team's base.

  • The Map : The battlefield is divided into two sides: The Radiant (bottom left) and The Dire (top right), separated by a river.
  • The Lanes: There are three main paths (Top, Middle, and Bottom) protected by defensive towers.
  • Economy & Growth: Players earn gold and experience by killing "creeps" (computer-controlled units), destroying structures, and defeating enemy heroes. This allows them to level up and purchase items to increase their power.
